Master of Information Management and Systems at [UC Berkeley's School of Information](https://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/) specializing in applied AI/ML.

Previously a Senior Technical Architect at [Accenture](https://www.accenture.com/id-en), where I spent 3+ years building cloud-native infrastructure and enterprise-scale systems serving 15M+ users. Proven track record of designing and deploying scalable data pipelines, optimizing distributed systems, and implementing CI/CD workflows in cloud environments.

My current work focuses on multimodal AI systems, generative models, and agentic workflows — bringing a strong software engineering and systems background to the design of reliable, data-driven AI applications.

Before Berkeley, I completed my bachelor in Computer Science at [Bandung Institute of Technology](https://itb.ac.id/about-itb), a journey that took me from writing my first lines of code to designing systems used by millions of users.

Outside of work, you'll usually find me discovering local coffee shops, exploring new places, practicing yoga, and cooking for friends. Some of my best ideas happen somewhere between a long walk and a good iced latte! ☕️👩🏻‍💻✨
